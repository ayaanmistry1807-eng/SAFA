About SAFA

Saeed Ahmed Football Academy (SAFA) is Maharashtra's premier destination for aspiring footballers. With state-of-the-art facilities and expert coaching led by Saeed Ahmed, we transform passion into professional excellence.

We focus on technical mastery, tactical intelligence, physical conditioning, and mental resilience — preparing players for national and international success.

Training Programs

Youth Foundation (6–12)

Fun-based skill development, ball mastery, and love for the game.

Elite Development (13–18)

Advanced tactics, strength & conditioning, match analysis.

Pro Pathway

Scouting exposure, trial preparation, professional mentorship.
